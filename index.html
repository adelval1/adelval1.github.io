Current research line

My work is focused on the determination of gas/surface interaction models for atmospheric entry protection materials when subjected to plasma flows. To achieve this goal, I propose to use novel uncertainty quantification techniques.

Science outreach

As scientists, we have the duty to help educate the general public about our findings and the impact that Science has in their lives. As a graduate student, I try to engage the public by showing them a common scientific view, one they can also relate to and learn from.
